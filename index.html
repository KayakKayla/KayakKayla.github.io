Joever Rover

Sept 2023 - Dec 2023

No need to fear, Joever Rover's here!

Feeling sad? Joever Rover can help cheer you up! This happy robot companion drives up to distressed people and plays music to bring joy wherever it goes! This was my SE 101 group project, and I mainly worked on Joever Rover's sight. I configured the camera module to work with OpenCV on the Raspberry Pi, eliminating the need to replace it with a usb camera. With the camera working, I implemented face detection using OpenCV. I also trained an emotion detection model with Keras so the Joever Rover knows who to go to.

School Map

Jan 2023 - May 2023

(insert)

At my secondary school, I noticed that many new students had trouble finding their classrooms. So in grade 12, I dedicated my capstone project to making a navigation app. I mapped out the entire school, then coded Dijkstra's algorithm in Python to find the shortest path from one classroom to another. Then, I used PIL to draw a path onto the map. To bring it all together, I used Kivy to create the app. For ease of use, I included a dropdown menu and scrollable map.
